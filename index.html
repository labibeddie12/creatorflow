FAQ

Before you book.

What is included in the ₹249 Reel edit?

Basic color grading, animated captions, and match cuts for one short-form Reel. Share your raw footage and any editing reference through Google Drive.

How do I send my raw videos?

Upload all raw clips, logos, music, and references to one Google Drive folder. Change access to “Anyone with the link can view,” then paste the link in the booking form.

Can I order 30 or more Reels?

Yes. Choose the 30-Reel or custom option and request a quote. The final rate depends on editing complexity, video length, content volume, and delivery schedule.

Do you provide services other than editing?

Yes. CreatorFlow can also help with content ideas, scripts, thumbnails, branding, posting support, profile optimization, and creator monetization.

How will I receive the completed videos?

Final files can be delivered through a Google Drive folder so you can preview and download them from any device.
